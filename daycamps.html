American Institute of Chemical Engineers - University of Iowa Student Chapter

The Fall Halloween themed day-camp will be held on: Sunday November 6th from 1-4pm

The AIChE Engineering Kids Day Camp is held bi-annually for children in kindergarten through fifth grade. The Fall Day Camp is Halloween themed, and is filled with activities including pumpkin decorating, crafting Dr. Seussâ€™s Oobleck, and concocting their very own slime! The Spring Day Camp is all about Earth Day and protecting our planet. The students engage in several activities that teach them about sustainability, being Green, and what they can do to be eco-friendly! The Day Camp is a 4-hour long event that is held on a Saturday or Sunday.

This event is organized by our Day Camp Coordinators, and run by volunteers from our AIChE student chapter that have completed a background check with the College of Engineering. There is a small participation fee, which helps us cover the cost of the supplies and raise some money for our chapter.
